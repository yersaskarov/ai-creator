# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity,
level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or
sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Encouraged behaviour:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respectful disagreement on technical ideas
- Accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its users

**Unacceptable behaviour:**

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported by contacting the project
maintainer at **yersultanaskarov@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed
confidentially.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
